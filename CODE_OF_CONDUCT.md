# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in TFWS project spaces must be professional, respectful, and
focused on improving the project.

Expected behavior includes:

- discussing technical disagreements with evidence and respect,
- giving actionable feedback without personal attacks,
- acknowledging uncertainty and correcting mistakes,
- protecting confidential and security-sensitive information,
- respecting different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, discrimination, or sexualized conduct,
- insults, trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith engagement,
- publishing another person's private information without permission,
- pressuring anyone to disclose secrets, credentials, or vulnerability details,
- retaliation against a good-faith reporter or contributor.

## Scope

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions,
security-reporting channels, and public interactions where someone represents
the TFWS project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `security@onetoo.eu` with the subject
`TFWS Code of Conduct`. Include links or evidence that can be shared safely.

The maintainer may edit or remove content, close or lock discussions, reject
contributions, issue a warning, or temporarily or permanently restrict
participation. Responses will consider context, severity, repetition, safety,
and the need to protect reporters.

Deliberately false or retaliatory reports are themselves a violation. Good-faith
reports will be handled as privately as reasonably possible.
